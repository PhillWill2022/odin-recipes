Pizza

An Italian dish with crust, tomato sauce, a blend of cheese, and pepperonis.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Pepperonis

Steps:

  1. Shape the dough into a flat circle.
  2. Spread tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan evenly on the sauce.
  4. Place pepperonis evenly across cheese.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees farenheit for 20 minutes.
