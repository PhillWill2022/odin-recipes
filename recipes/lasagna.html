Lasagna

An Italian dish filled with layers of pasta, meat, and red sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Red Pasta Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Herbs & Spices

Steps:

  1. Boil lasagna noodles.
  2. Brown ground beef.
  3. Layer pasta, ground beef, and red sauce.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees farenheit for 45 minutes.
